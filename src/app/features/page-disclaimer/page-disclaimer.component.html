Before we get started

We use an artificial intelligence model to determine whether a person is at risk of suffering from a heart condition. This means that although we strive to be as accurate as possible, we cannot offer medical advice. If you think your health may be at risk or you are suffering from dangerous symptoms, please consult a medical practitioner.

You'll be asked to fill in various medical details. We will feed this information into our AI-model and use this to determine if you are at risk of a heart disease. We will not store your personal information or share this with third-parties.
