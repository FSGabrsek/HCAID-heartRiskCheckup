The model

Our online heart risk check-up relies on a random forest model, which is a complex algorithm designed to predict your risk of heart-related issues. It considers several important factors in this assessment, with three key ones being the type of chest pain you may experience, your maximum achieved heart rate, and the results of your fluoroscopy test.

The following ranking shows how important each factor is in our assessment, helping you understand which ones matter most in our decision-making process.
